Comment Policy

Welcome to SEO Tool Mule.com.

We welcome new conversations and discussions on our site about SEO and your own thoughts on what we post.

We hope you enjoy what our site has to offer and find something which assists you in world of SEO optimization.

There is a comment section avaiable on each blog post, which is open for any one to post to.

Before you comment or add to the discussion, we ask that you review our rules and guidlines for commenting on our site below.

How to comment

SEO Tool Mule.com currently utilizes Disqus' comment application. To comment on our pages, you can either post as a guest, or sign-in with an official free Disqus account.

We appreciate thoughtful and civilized discussion, however we reserves the right to edit or remove comments as we see fit, without explanation.

What your comments should include

  • Use your real first name and email address in the comment form
  • Add smart, relevant ideas that expand on an article’s premise — read the article before you comment
  • Offer useful, constructive criticism if necessary

Comments that do not adhere to these guidelines may be deleted.

What not to include

  • Spammy or irrelevant comments
  • Off-topic or personal messages
  • Threats to us or other visitors
  • Any form of defamation
  • Racist or obscene nastiness
  • Violation of anyone’s copyright, trademark, etc.
  • Promotions about and/or links back to your product, service, or latest venture

Please be respectful of people and the community here, and think before you post.

Additional comment guidelines and disclaimers

All rights reserved: For any comment, we reserve the right to edit, delete, move, or mark it as spam. We also reserve the right to block any IP address that violates this comment policy from commenting, subscribing, or accessing SEO Tool Mule.

Links: If you must include a link in your comment, please be aware that your comment may not be approved for posting. Be patient. Relevant links that contribute to the conversation need to be approved. Obvious attempts to promote your business, gain backlinks, traffic, or other types of self-promotion are not allowed.

Liability: By posting a comment, you agree that your contribution is your own and that you take responsibility for it. You agree to hold SEO Tool Mule, all post authors, and other commenters harmless.

Copyright: If we receive authentic notice that a comment is in violation of anyone’s copyrighted information, the comment will be deleted and the commenter may be blocked from posting comments in the future.

Spam: Don’t do it. Any comment deemed as spam will be deleted and marked as spam. If you repeatedly post spam, your IP address may be blacklisted from accessing SEO Tool Mule.

Privacy: You need to provide an authentic, verified email address to post a comment on SEO Tool Mule and receive comment subscriptions. We will never publish your personal information on the blog. Accordingly, do not share private information within your comment. We will delete phone numbers, email addresses, or any other private or personal information to protect the affected party. This is not the place to exchange contact information with other visitors.

Patience: If you follow this comment policy, your comment should be posted. Please be patient while we moderate our conversations to ensure the best experiences for our site visitors.
